Sorivo story

A calmer kind of flow.

The name Sorivo comes from softness, life, and flow — a quietly powerful state where your mind feels clear and your body feels steady.

The name Sorivo is built from three roots:

  • “Sore” – softness, serenity
  • “Vivo” – life, vitality, living
  • “Rivo” – river or flow in several Romance languages

Together, Sorivo represents a flowing, serene vitality — a calm, focused state that still feels fully alive.

Sorivo – AM is built to reflect that idea: modern wellness with minimal effort, and supplements that respect your brain and body over the long term.
